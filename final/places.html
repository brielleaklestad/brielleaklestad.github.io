Flathead lake offers hotels, Air Bnb's, and rental cabins for lodging. The pricing ranges anywhere from $85 to $300+ in the busy season. While it is more affordable to stay in the off season, many of the recreational activities are only possible in the summertime busy season. Each of these accomodations offers a wide selection of room types to fulfill your needs. One of the local favorites is The Flathead Lake Lodge. Located about 4 miles south of Bigfork, MT, they offer cabins, motel rooms, and even dry vintage campers. They are known for their impeccable customer service, private beach access, and their close proximity to Glacier National Park, Swan river, and Flathead river.
